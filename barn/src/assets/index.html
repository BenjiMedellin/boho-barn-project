About Us:

Bodark Barn was built in 2015 for our own wedding.
Now, we want to share our beautiful barn with you!

We like to consider ourselves more of a backyard wedding and event center.
We are rustic, industrial, boho, and repurposed. We pride ourselves in being a DIY place.
This allows you to really make your event your own and save a little money as well!
We even offer a horse drawn carriage and animal additions to make your event unique!

Let us host your next event!!
